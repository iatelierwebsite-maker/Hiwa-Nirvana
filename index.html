Frequently Asked Questions

What are the villa sizes available?

We offer 3 BHK (2800 Sft) and 4 BHK (3200-4500 Sft) independent villas.

Is the project HMDA approved?

Yes, Hiwa Nirvana is fully HMDA approved and RERA registered.

What is the completion timeline?

Phase 1 is ready for handover by Dec 2025. Phase 2 by mid-2026.

Are banks providing loans?

Yes, the project is approved by SBI, HDFC, ICICI, and Axis Bank.

Can we customize the interiors?

Yes, internal structural changes are permitted before the brickwork stage.
