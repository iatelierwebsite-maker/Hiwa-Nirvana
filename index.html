Frequently Asked Questions

What are the villa sizes available?

We offer 3 BHK (2800 Sft) and 4 BHK (3200-4500 Sft) independent villas.

What is the completion timeline?

Phase 1 is ready for handover by Dec 2025. Phase 2 by mid-2026.
